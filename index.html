The New Era

First and foremost we are gamers. Our platform was designed and built from the perspective of a multitude of avid gamers- this is a knowledge that is currently lacking in the eSports market. We digitize the physical tournament environment by providing the player with easy to use tools to act as their own team manager, event organizer, and tournament creator. Everything associated with a tournament can be managed from within our hub.

Our goal is to empower the player, allowing them to break free from the centralized eSports market that is taking shape. With our platform, we focus on the gaming experience but integrate with current spectator side technology.

To the pros- STOP giving your tournament winnings to corporate team owners and managers- we simplify and automate this process, allowing winners to keep all of their winnings.
